How to play on desktop

Running & jumping: The player can run left and right by pressing the left and right arrow keys. The character will jump if the up-arrow is pressed.

Shooting: The player can shoot by left clicking their mouse.

Shooting a flameball: The player can shoot a flameball by right clicking their mouse.
